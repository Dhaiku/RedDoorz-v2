Customer

Browse, book, pay, review

Step 1
Browse Homepage
Featured hotels (top rated) + Browse by Location city cards
index.php
↓
Step 2
Search Hotels
Filter by city/name + optional date range. PHP-side filtering from Firestore results.
hotels/search.php
↓
Step 3
View Hotel Detail
Rooms list, availability check, guest reviews, hotel info
hotels/hotel_detail.php
↓
Step 4
Book a Room
Select dates & guests. Overlap check vs existing bookings + blocked dates. Generate ref code.
hotels/book.php
↓
Step 5
Payment
Online (GCash/Card) → confirmed + earnings record created. Walk-in → stays pending.
hotels/payment.php
↓
Step 6
My Bookings Dashboard
View booking history, statuses, booking details
customer/dashboard.php
↓
Step 7
Leave a Review
After stay completes, submit star rating + comment on hotel
customer/booking_detail.php
🏨

Hotel Owner

Manage hotel, rooms, bookings

Step 1
Apply as Partner
Submit hotel name, city, room count, contact. Creates ownerapplications record.
owner/apply.php
↓
Step 2
Admin Approval
Admin reviews application, creates owner account + hotel record, sends credentials
admin/manage_owners.php
↓
Step 3
Owner Dashboard
Overview: today's bookings, earnings snapshot, occupancy stats
owner/dashboard.php
↓
Step 4
Manage Rooms Own hotel only
Add/edit/delete rooms for their hotel only. Set type, price, capacity. Block dates for maintenance.
owner/manage_rooms.php
↓
Step 5
Handle Bookings Own hotel only
Confirm walk-in payments, mark check-in/check-out, cancel — scoped to their hotel's bookings only.
owner/manage_bookings.php
↓
Step 6
Track Earnings
View earnings per booking. 85% owner share / 15% platform fee split.
owner/earnings.php
↓
Step 7
Manage Staff
Add front desk staff with roles and contact info for the hotel
owner/manage_staff.php
🛡️

Admin

Platform oversight & control

Step 1
Dashboard Overview
Total bookings, revenue, cancellation rate, avg rating, pending payments alert
admin/dashboard.php
↓
Step 2
Manage Hotels
Activate/deactivate hotels, edit details, monitor all properties
admin/manage_hotels.php
↓
Step 3
Manage Rooms Platform-wide
Oversee all rooms across every hotel. Filter by hotel, update status. Admin-only view — not limited to one property.
admin/manage_rooms.php
↓
Step 4
Manage Bookings Platform-wide
View/filter all bookings across every hotel. Override statuses, flag issues. Different from owner view which is hotel-scoped.
admin/manage_bookings.php
↓
Step 5
Manage Customers
View all customer accounts, booking history, suspend accounts
admin/manage_customers.php
↓
Step 6
Manage Hotel Owners
Review applications, approve/reject, create owner accounts
admin/manage_owners.php
↓
Step 7
Manage Payouts
Review payout requests from owners, approve or reject with notes
admin/manage_payouts.php
